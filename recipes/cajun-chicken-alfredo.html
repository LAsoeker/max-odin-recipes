Cajun Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Nice Chicken Alfredo with creamy Pasta

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken breast
  • 300g Pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 gloves minced garlic
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • fresh parsley

Seasoning

  • 2 tsp each smoked paprika, parlic powder, onion powder
  • 1tsb salt and pepper
  • 1/2 tsb cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic herb blend seasoning

Directions

  1. Season chicken
  2. Cook chicken in skillet and flip 3 mins per side. Set aside
  3. Add butter and garlic to the pan
  4. Add chicken broth, cream ,corn starch and Parmesan cheese
  5. Boil noodles then add to pan and thickened sauce
  6. Top with sliced chicken and parsley
